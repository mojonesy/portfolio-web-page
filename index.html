My Name is Molly

Hello - Thanks for visiting my portfolio page! My name is Molly Jones. I am a professional musician and aspring software developer based out of Buffalo, NY. I am currently a student in Thinkful's Software Engineering Flex Program, and am enjoying learning about coding and web development while continuing to create music.

Although I've only just begun my software development journey, I've already realized some of the many similarities that musicians and developers share:

  1. Endless creative potential
  2. A passionate drive to hone their craft
  3. An ability to effectively collaborate with each other
  4. A shared interest in the devlopment of their industry

Drawing these conclusions has made me even more eager and excited to keep learning and developing my skills, as I realize the creativity that my future projects can hold in connection with my passion for music.
